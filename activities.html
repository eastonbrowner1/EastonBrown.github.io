The activities at Pacific Trails

hiking

Hike along the beautiful pacific coast and watch the waves hit the the sand. Or hike into the forest with beautiful plants and wildlife

kayaking

Kayak on the Pacific River with some of cleanest water in California with beautiful rapids and slow to fast flowing water that dumps into the pacific ocean

Bird Watching

Watch some of the most unique birds fly across the beach. including eagles, falcons, and owls. One set of binoculars will be handed to each person to watch the birds.
